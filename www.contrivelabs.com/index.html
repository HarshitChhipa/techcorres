How we started

Why not? We were left wondering while using some products | experiencing a service | working for corporates | and most importantly, talking to users.

Those questions pointed to only two things - learn to live with it -or- BUILD!

The Choice was obvious!

We pride ourselves being a product company that solves problems creatively & make a meaningful difference to the users.

Who are we?

We are an ingenious team that builds great solutions!

Want to make lives of people easy - free the human brain of unwanted processing - rather use it to change the world for better

Aim to solve problems with our products/solutions that are so human oriented, that the problem will be forgotten

Set high standards and we compete with ourselves - with every product that we develop, challenging the status quo of unintuitive practices

We believe no problem is too hard to solve - if we use the stuff between the ears
